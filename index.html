Jungle City

The Free City of Jungle City is an autonomous city located on Earth. It is considered a modern multicultural and thriving metropolis of the 69th-century, overrun by corporations, corruption, organized crime and gang violence. Jungle City was founded by a group of united free monkeys, it was originally known as Monkey City before its renaming in 2021.
